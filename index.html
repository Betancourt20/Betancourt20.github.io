About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Julio Betancourt

I am Julio Betancourt. Currently, I am a postdoctoral fellow at ONERA (The French Aerospace Lab). I am working with a robot manipulator for enabling and guiding a safety Human-robot interaction (HRI).

My main research interest is in Automatic control. I am thrilled for Nonlinear, linear control, Robust control, UDE (Uncertainty Disturbance Observer), SMC (Sliding Mode Control), Visual Servoing and Model Predictive Control (MPC).

I was born in Coahuila, Mexico, in 1991. I received a PhD in Automatic and Robotics in 2021 from the University of Technology of Compiègne, France; a MSc in Automatic Control in 2016 from the CENIDET (National Center for Research and Technological Development), Cuernavaca, Mexico; and a BSc in Mechatronics in 2012 from the Polytechnic University of Victoria city, Mexico.
